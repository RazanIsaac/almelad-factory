About Al-Melad Factory

Al-Melad Factory, founded by Khader Ishaq in the heart of Bethlehem, crafts spiritual and artistic treasures from authentic olive wood. Our collection includes rosaries and bracelets that connect faith, tradition, and beauty.

Every piece is handmade with care, passed through generations of craftsmanship inspired by centuries-old Christian heritage.
